North, Lehmann and Dunbrack, Supplemental Data


1. supplClusterData.txt -- contains information on median structures
of each of our clusters, including chain and residue positions

2. supplementalData.txt -- contains information on each loop in our
dataset, its cluster membership, its chain and residue positions, its
sequence and geometric string

3. supplClusterDataH3AnchorNterm3Cterm4.txt -- contains information on median structures
of each of our H3-anchor clusters, including chain and residue positions

4. supplementalDataH3AnchorNterm3Cterm4.txt -- contains information on each loop in our
dataset for H3-anchor regions, its cluster membership, its chain and residue positions, its
sequence and geometric string
